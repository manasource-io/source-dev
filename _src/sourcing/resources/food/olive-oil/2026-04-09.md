# Olive Oil — Research Notes 2026-04-09

- **Resource**: `resources/nutrition/food/olive-oil.md`

## Claims Verified

### 28% Dementia-Related Death Reduction
PubMed 38709531 — Tessier et al. JAMA Network Open 2024, Harvard-led prospective cohort of 92,383 participants over 28 years — fully confirmed. The 28% reduction (for more than 7g/day vs. rare/no consumption) is accurately stated in the resource. The JAMA Network Open full text and NIA summary are both still accessible and consistent.

### Cognitive Function / Cognitive Decline
MDPI Nutrients RCT (mdpi.com/2072-6643/14/23/5102): confirmed valid, supports blood-brain barrier and cognitive claims in mild cognitive impairment patients.
PMC6825477: confirmed valid, secoiridoid/oleuropein mechanism review, supports neuroprotective claims.

### Cardiovascular Protection
The body text listed cardiovascular protection as a benefit but the existing prose focused almost entirely on dementia/cognitive outcomes. PREDIMED and CORDIOPREV RCT evidence (surfaced in search results) directly supports the cardiovascular-protection benefit. Added a paragraph to the body and added PMC10856527 as a reference covering this evidence domain.

### New Reference Added
PMC10856527 — Alkhalifa et al. 2024, Int J Mol Sci — comprehensive review covering cellular, animal, and clinical EVOO studies in Alzheimer's disease. Covers amyloid-beta, tau, neuroinflammation, BBB mechanisms. Moderate credibility (review paper). Fills in mechanistic detail and is the most thorough recent synthesis across study types.
