## Claims Reviewed

Reddit post (1mwq6g5, August 2025, 658 upvotes) confirming PREDIMED trial, polyphenol compounds (oleocanthal, hydroxytyrosol), and cardiovascular claims already present in the resource.

Key new insight from top comment (81 upvotes): polyphenols in olive oil degrade within 6-9 months of pressing. Early-harvest olives produce higher polyphenol content; polyphenols are destroyed by sunlight exposure. Dark glass or metal tins required. The throat peppery sensation (oleocanthal) is a direct quality indicator. Large commercial brands blend multiple sources to meet minimum legal standards, often with borderline polyphenol levels.
