# Apple Cider Vinegar — Research Notes

- **Resource**: `resources/nutrition/food/apple-cider-vinegar.md`
- **Leads**: WebSearch — 2025 GRADE meta-analyses on glycemic control and body composition

## Claims Reviewed

Existing references (PMID 37608660 and 31451249) remain valid and pertinent.

New 2025 research corroborates the glycemic control claims:
- **PMID 39949546** (Frontiers in Nutrition 2025): GRADE-assessed meta-analysis of 7 trials confirming ~22 mg/dL FBG and ~1.5% HbA1c reductions. Dose-response curve favors intakes above 10 mL/day.
- **MDPI Nutrients 2025**: Meta-analysis of 10 RCTs showing body weight and waist circumference reductions with daily ACV; effect sizes modest (1–3 kg), GRADE certainty moderate.
