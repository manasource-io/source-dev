# Apple Cider Vinegar — Research Notes

- **Resource**: `resources/nutrition/food/apple-cider-vinegar.md`
- **Leads**: General source-check pass (no Reddit lead)

## Claims Reviewed

The existing body described ACV improving insulin sensitivity and lowering blood sugar responses — this is well-supported by current evidence, but the only reference was a Healthline consumer article, not a primary source. Two peer-reviewed meta-analyses were found and added.

Blood sugar claims are substantiated: a 2023 meta-analysis of 25 RCTs (PMID 37608660) shows meaningful HbA1c and fasting glucose reductions. A 2019 RCT (PMID 31451249) confirms improvements in HOMA-IR and oxidative stress. The effect sizes are real but modest.

Weight management claims are more mixed — some RCTs show weight reductions but effect sizes vary widely and confounding is a concern.

Antimicrobial claims are supported in vitro; significance in vivo is uncertain.

## Notes

`benefitLevel: 4` reflects that ACV's benefits are real but limited in scope and magnitude — primarily a modest adjunct for blood sugar management rather than a major health intervention. Credibility is `moderate` because the RCTs are numerous but often small and heterogeneous. Would benefit from a larger high-quality RCT specifically powered for HbA1c endpoints.
