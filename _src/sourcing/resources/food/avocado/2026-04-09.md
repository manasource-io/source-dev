## New Research Found

### PubMed 39641169 — Avocado and Gut Microbiota (HAT Ancillary, 2025)
- **Study**: HAT RCT ancillary; 1,008 adults with abdominal obesity; 26 weeks; shotgun metagenomics
- **Findings**: Daily avocado increased alpha and beta diversity; enriched *Faecalibacterium prausnitzii* (SCFA producer, anti-inflammatory); benefits strongest in participants with low baseline diet quality (HEI under 52.7)
- **Action**: Added new "Gut Microbiome" section to avocado.md; added to references and source-checks.csv

### PubMed 39968784 — Avocado and Life's Essential 8 (HAT Ancillary, 2025)
- **Study**: HAT RCT ancillary; 969 adults; 26 weeks; JAHA
- **Findings**: Daily avocado improved LE8 subscores for diet quality (+3.53), sleep health (+3.20), and blood lipids (+3.46); overall CVD composite did not reach significance
- **Credibility**: Moderate — pre-specified secondary outcomes; null primary result tempers interpretation
- **Action**: Mentioned in the new Gut Microbiome section alongside the microbiota findings; added to references and source-checks.csv
