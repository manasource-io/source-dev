## Claims Reviewed

Critical issue found: the existing resource listed a Reddit post URL as a reference. Reddit posts are leads, not sources — removed and replaced with focus on actual peer-reviewed studies already in the reference list.

The Reddit post title claims "significantly increase lifespan" — this is an overstatement based on the actual evidence. The peer-reviewed literature supports cardiovascular risk reduction and cholesterol improvement, which are associated with reduced mortality, but no direct RCT on lifespan has been conducted.

Claims verified:

- **Cardiovascular risk**: Harvard prospective cohort (110k+ participants, 30y) shows 16% lower CVD risk with 2+ servings/week. Well-supported.
- **Cholesterol**: Multiple meta-analyses confirm reductions in total cholesterol and LDL, particularly in hypercholesterolemic individuals.
- **Nutrient absorption**: Well-documented effect — fat content dramatically increases carotenoid absorption from co-consumed vegetables.
- **Body weight**: Neutral effect on body weight confirmed across RCTs. Calorie density does not lead to weight gain when substituted for other foods.
- **Anti-inflammatory**: Oleic acid, phytosterols, and avocado-soybean unsaponifiables (ASU) have demonstrated anti-inflammatory effects.
