## Claims Reviewed

Reddit post (868 upvotes) reported a Columbia University study showing cocoa flavanols reversed age-related memory loss. The first comment identified the source as Brickman et al. 2014, Nature Neuroscience — confirmed via PubMed (PMID 25344629, PMC4940121). Study enrolled 50–69 yr-olds in a 3-month high-vs-low flavanol RCT; high-flavanol group showed significantly improved dentate gyrus blood volume and memory scores.

Two previously validated but unlinked references (from April 2026 source-checks) were added to the resource:
- PMID 41397115: theobromine and epigenetic aging (Aging-US 2025, observational, n=1669)
- PMID 40832703: COSMOS sub-analysis on blood pressure (Hypertension 2025)

Existing 3 references re-confirmed valid.
