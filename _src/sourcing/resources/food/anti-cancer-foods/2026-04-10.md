# Anti-Cancer Foods — Research Notes

- **Resource**: Multiple (see Changes Made)
- **Leads**: r/immortalists post by Dr. Georgios Ioannou — "Best Anti-Cancer Foods. These foods significantly fight cancer" (reddit.com/r/immortalists/comments/1sh1j4b/)

## Claims Reviewed

The post provides an accurate lay summary of cancer-preventive foods with 12 peer-reviewed references. Key mechanisms covered: sulforaphane/cancer stem cell apoptosis, anthocyanin/ellagic acid anti-angiogenesis, resveratrol cell cycle arrest, curcumin tumor pathway disruption, piperine bioavailability synergy, garlic/onion organosulfur detoxification, EGCG tumor growth interference, lycopene prostate/breast protection, omega-3/polyphenol cancer suppression, lignan tumor proliferation inhibition, olive oil polyphenol anti-cancer activity.

All 12 cited references were verified as peer-reviewed and pertinent.

## New Attributes Added to Schema

- **`pairing`**: array of companion resources/conditions that enhance benefits (`requisite` or `synergy` type)
- **`conflicts`**: array of items/conditions that reduce efficacy or should be avoided (`absorption-blocker`, `interaction`, `timing` types)
- Both documented in `research/workflow.md` with examples
