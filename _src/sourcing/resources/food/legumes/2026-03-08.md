## Claims Reviewed

The Reddit fiber post specifically highlights lentils, black beans, and kidney beans as top fiber-rich foods for longevity. Legumes as a category are among the highest fiber-density foods and are a primary driver of the fiber-mortality association seen in large cohort studies.

Mechanism is dual: soluble fiber → cholesterol reduction; prebiotic fiber → SCFA production and gut microbiome support.
