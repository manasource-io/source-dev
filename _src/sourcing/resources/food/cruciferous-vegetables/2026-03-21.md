## Claims Reviewed

Original claims were accurate but understated the emerging longevity angle. The 2025 _bioRxiv_ study by Sutphin et al. (PubMed: 40462948) showed sulforaphane extends _C. elegans_ lifespan by 50%+ and reduces transcriptional biological age by ~20% — a significant finding that justifies higher `benefitLevel`.

NCI source correctly notes that human evidence for cancer prevention remains mixed despite strong mechanistic data.

Added new reference: https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/40462948/ (sulforaphane lifespan extension study, 2025).
