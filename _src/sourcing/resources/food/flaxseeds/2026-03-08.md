## Claims Reviewed

Flaxseeds are mentioned in the Reddit fiber post as a high-fiber seed. Key nutritional facts are well-established: richest dietary lignan source, high ALA content, mixed soluble/insoluble fiber. The key practical note — grind before eating — is critical and well-supported (whole seeds pass through largely undigested).

Direct flaxseed RCT evidence for mortality reduction is limited. Credibility set to `moderate` accordingly.
