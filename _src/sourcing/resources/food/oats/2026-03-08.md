## Claims Reviewed

Oats are specifically mentioned in the Reddit fiber post as an excellent fiber source. Beta-glucan (oat's primary soluble fiber) has particularly strong RCT evidence for LDL reduction — it's one of the few dietary fibers with an FDA-approved health claim for cholesterol reduction.
