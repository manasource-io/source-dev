# Nuts — Research Notes

- **Resource**: `resources/nutrition/food/nuts.md`
- **Leads**: Prior Reddit post (r/Immortalists 1lkm11v) titled "nuts significantly increase lifespan"; PubMed search for nuts mortality longevity 2024

## Claims Reviewed

The original resource had 14 references but a body of only ~250 characters — the opposite problem of most stubs (lots of references, almost no content). Eight of the 14 references were either Reddit posts, media articles (Time, Washington Post, Yahoo Health), or an industry aggregator page, and were removed. The four PubMed references are all valid and strong.

Claims confirmed by evidence:

- All-cause mortality reduction: well-supported (walnut study with 93k adults over 20 years; tree nut review in 55+ cohort; ASPREE disability-free survival study in 10k adults 70+)
- Cardiovascular benefit: very strong; multiple mechanisms; meta-analyses consistently show LDL/TG reduction
- Healthy aging / disability-free survival: 2024 ASPREE study provides direct evidence
- Cognitive health: emerging, inconclusive; walnuts most studied; not ready to claim definitively
