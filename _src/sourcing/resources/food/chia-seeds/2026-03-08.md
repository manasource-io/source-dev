## Claims Reviewed

Chia seeds are listed in the Reddit fiber post as a top fiber source. At ~10g fiber per ounce, they are exceptionally dense. However, direct RCT evidence for chia seeds specifically improving mortality or cardiovascular outcomes is limited; most evidence is inferred from their fiber and omega-3 (ALA) content. ALA from plant sources has weaker cardiovascular evidence than marine omega-3s (EPA/DHA).

Credibility set to `moderate` to reflect this distinction.
