## Claims Reviewed

Black beans are specifically named in the Reddit fiber post as a top longevity food. They are a legume, so they share the strong mortality-reduction evidence from large cohort studies of legume consumption. Their anthocyanin (dark pigment) content is a bonus anti-inflammatory benefit. All claims well-supported.
