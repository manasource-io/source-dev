## Claims Reviewed

The original resource body was sparse (~400 characters) with a single reference — a Mayo Clinic recipe page that returned a 404. No frontmatter attributes were set. The resource had no established benefits, credibility rating, or readiness status.

Research confirms pumpkin is well-supported as a nutrient-dense antioxidant food. The carotenoid content (beta-carotene dominant) is exceptionally high and supported by multiple 2024–2025 peer-reviewed reviews. Specific health benefits are documented in animal models and observational studies: blood glucose regulation, liver protection, lipid improvement, and antioxidant activity. Human RCT evidence for individual outcomes (beyond antioxidant capacity) is limited — moderate credibility is appropriate.
