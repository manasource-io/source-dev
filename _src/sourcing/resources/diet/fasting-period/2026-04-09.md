## Key Findings

- 2025 network meta-analysis (56 RCTs): IF reduces weight, LDL, BP, fasting glucose vs usual diet; modified ADF most effective for BP
- 2025 Nutrition Journal meta-analysis (15 RCTs): body fat, LDL, fasting glucose reductions confirmed
- 2025 Nature Communications RCT: 8% weight loss, 16% body fat reduction, significant LDL and triglyceride improvements over 6 months
- Lancet umbrella review: head-to-head vs caloric restriction shows comparable short-term outcomes — IF works but may not outperform equivalent CR
- ChronoFast 2025: isocaloric TRE shifts circadian clocks but limited cardiometabolic improvement, suggesting spontaneous calorie reduction explains part of benefit

## Credibility Assessment

- benefitLevel set to 7: solid cardiometabolic evidence, practical adherence benefits, but effect sizes moderate and not clearly independent of calorie restriction
