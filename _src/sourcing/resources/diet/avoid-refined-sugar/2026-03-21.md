# Avoid Refined Sugar — Research Notes

- **Resource**: `resources/nutrition/diet/avoid-refined-sugar.md`
- **Leads**: Web search for sugar intake, CVD mortality, dose-response meta-analyses 2022–2024

## Claims Reviewed

- CVD mortality: confirmed — 2023 meta-analysis (Huang et al.): total sugar RR 1.10 CVD mortality; fructose RR 1.11; non-linear dose-response
- All-cause mortality: confirmed — same meta-analysis: total sugar RR 1.09; fructose RR 1.09
- T2D and CHD (SSBs): confirmed — Li et al. 2023 meta-analysis: monotonic dose-response for SSBs and T2D, CHD, stroke, all-cause mortality; RR 1.08–1.54
- Gut barrier disruption: confirmed — PMID 34902573: refined sugars disrupt tight junctions, drive dysbiosis and mucosal immune activation
- Systemic inflammation: confirmed — de novo lipogenesis, AGE formation, LPS translocation — multiple mechanistic pathways
- Blood pressure: confirmed — fructose → uric acid → inhibits nitric oxide → raises BP
