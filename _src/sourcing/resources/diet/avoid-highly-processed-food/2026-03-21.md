# Avoid Highly Processed Food — Research Notes

- **Resource**: `resources/nutrition/diet/avoid-highly-processed-food.md`
- **Leads**: Web search for UPF meta-analyses 2024–2025

## Claims Reviewed

- All-cause mortality: confirmed — 2025 meta-analysis (18 studies, 1.1M participants): HR=1.15 highest vs lowest; +10% per 10% increment in UPF calories
- CVD mortality: confirmed — BMJ umbrella review: non-dose-response RR 1.50; Lancet Americas cohorts: significant CVD, CHD, stroke associations
- T2D: convincing evidence per BMJ umbrella review — RR 1.12 per unit dose
- Anxiety + mental disorders: convincing evidence per BMJ umbrella review — OR 1.48–1.53; exceptional finding for a dietary pattern
- UPF linked to 71% of 45 health parameters studied — broadest dietary harm signal in modern epidemiology
- Obesity, sleep quality, respiratory (wheezing): "highly suggestive evidence" in umbrella review
