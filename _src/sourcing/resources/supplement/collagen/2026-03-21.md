## Claims Reviewed

The existing stub included the claim that collagen supplementation supports "the structural integrity of the gut lining." This claim was NOT supported by the Harvard Nutrition Source reference in the file — Harvard's page does not discuss gut lining effects of supplemental collagen. This claim was removed from the updated resource body.

The skin elasticity and joint pain claims are well-supported. A 2025 meta-analysis of 23 RCTs confirmed skin improvements but found weaker effects in industry-independent studies. A 2025 npj Aging paper (ETH Zürich, DOI: 10.1038/s41514-025-00280-7) shows a specific collagen amino acid ratio reduces biological age in humans — a notable new finding added to the resource.
