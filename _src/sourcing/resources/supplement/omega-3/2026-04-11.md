## Claims Reviewed

The harvest post restates the Framingham Offspring cohort finding (~5 years longer life expectancy for highest omega-3 index) accurately. The post is anecdote-heavy but points to real underlying evidence already captured in the resource.

Source check identified a misidentified reference: PMID 23746276 was previously labeled in source-checks.csv as "Rizos et al. 2012 JAMA meta-analysis" but is actually Crupi et al. 2013 on n-3 fatty acids and neuroplasticity. The Rizos paper is PMID 22968891. Both references are legitimate — 23746276 supports the brain health section; 22968891 supports the cardiovascular nuance section.
