## Scoring Rationale

- **benefitLevel: 5** — Animal evidence is exceptionally strong (ITP replication across species); human immune data is meaningful; but absence of human longevity RCTs caps this well below supplements with direct human evidence
- **overallScore: 3** — Applied `round(benefitLevel × 0.65)` per credibility:emerging rule; reflects the genuine uncertainty of translation from mice to humans
- **credibility: emerging** — Mechanistic rationale is sound, animal data is the best of any longevity drug, but human evidence for the primary claim (longevity) is absent; immune data is the exception but a narrower benefit
