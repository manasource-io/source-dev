# Ashwagandha — Research Notes

- **Leads**: Web search on ashwagandha systematic reviews 2024–2025; PubMed meta-analyses

## Claims Reviewed

Original resource was a stub with accurate but minimal content. Both existing references are valid:

- examine.com: strong, 137 references across 36 trials and 4 meta-analyses
- PubMed 23439798: valid RCT (2012), 64 adults, significant cortisol and PSS reduction

New 2025 meta-analyses confirm and strengthen the cortisol-reduction claim:

- PMC12242034: 15 RCTs, 873 patients, significant cortisol and HAM-A anxiety reductions
- PubMed 40746175: significant cortisol reduction (−1.16 µg/dL), but mixed results on PSS

The dual finding — strong cortisol reduction, mixed PSS results — is an important nuance that distinguishes biological from subjective stress relief.
