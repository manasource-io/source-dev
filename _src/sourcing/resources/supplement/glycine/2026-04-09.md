## Claims Reviewed

| Claim | Verdict | Notes |
|-------|---------|-------|
| 3g before bed improves subjective sleep quality | Supported | PMC3328957: RCT confirms reduced sleep latency, improved quality and next-day alertness |
| Mechanism: peripheral vasodilation via SCN NMDA receptors lowers core body temperature | Supported | PMC4397399: mechanistic study directly confirms this pathway |
| Polysomnographic improvements (slow-wave sleep) | Supported | Reported in PMC3328957 alongside subjective measures |
| Glycine is most abundant amino acid in collagen | Supported | Well-established biochemistry; ~1/3 of collagen by mass |
| Endogenous synthesis provides only ~3g/day vs ~10–15g needed | Supported | PMC11510825 and Examine both cite this gap |
| May improve insulin sensitivity and metabolic markers | Plausible / limited | Examine notes mechanistic basis and observational data; intervention evidence thin |
| Very well tolerated, no established upper limit | Supported | Consistent across all sources reviewed |

## What Was Written and Why

### Frontmatter
- `benefitLevel: 5` — Real, replicated sleep effects in small RCTs, plus well-understood collagen role; falls short of strong because primary sleep trials are small (n=11) and the metabolic evidence is weak
- `overallScore: 4` — Round(5 × 0.85) = 4.25, rounded down to 4; credibility is moderate given small RCT sample sizes
- `credibility: moderate` — Sleep RCTs are placebo-controlled and mechanistically coherent but underpowered; collagen claim is biochemically strong but clinical outcomes are less directly measured

### Sections written
- **Sleep Quality** — Covers the primary RCT evidence (PMC3328957) with specific outcome measures
- **Core Body Temperature Pathway** — Explains the NMDA/SCN/vasodilation mechanism (PMC4397399); this is unusually well-characterized for a sleep supplement and worth highlighting in detail
- **Collagen Synthesis** — Covers the endogenous synthesis gap and supplementation rationale; relevant to the physical recovery angle in PMC11510825
- **Metabolic Health** — Included as a third benefit with appropriate hedging; mechanistically plausible but lower confidence than the other two
- **Dosing** — 3g pre-sleep (RCT-validated) and 5–15g for collagen (consistent with Examine and review literature); safety note included
