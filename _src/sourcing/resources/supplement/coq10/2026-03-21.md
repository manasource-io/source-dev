## Claims Reviewed

- Cardiovascular health: strongly supported — Q-SYMBIO trial (42% MACE reduction), Swedish selenium+CoQ10 RCT (5.9% vs 12.6% CV mortality)
- Mitochondrial energy: well-mechanized; CoQ10 is structurally essential to the electron transport chain
- Blood pressure: 2025 meta-analysis of 45 RCTs confirms −3.44 mmHg systolic reduction
- Statin protection: 2024 meta-analysis supports use for SAMS; low-risk adjunct
- Anti-aging/longevity directly: debated; mitohormesis concerns limit enthusiasm for blanket use in healthy adults
- Cognitive benefits: mixed results; 2025 review notes ongoing trials, no definitive conclusion yet
