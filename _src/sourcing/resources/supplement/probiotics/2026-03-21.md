# Probiotics — Research Notes

- **Resource**: `resources/nutrition/supplements/probiotics.md`
- **Leads**: Web search for gut microbiome and longevity research 2024–2025

## Claims Reviewed

- Gut health (GI conditions): strong evidence for antibiotic-associated diarrhea prevention (-51%), modest IBS benefit; strain-specific
- Microbiome diversity: 2025 meta-analysis (29 RCTs older adults) confirms increased Bifidobacterium and Shannon diversity index
- Inflammation/aging: 2025 evidence links SCFA-producing bacteria to NF-κB suppression, reduced SASP, anti-inflammaging
- Immune support: synbiotic (L. rhamnosus GG + fiber) improves NK cell activity in elderly women
- Longevity: centenarian microbiome research shows high diversity and Bifidobacterium enrichment — correlation, not proven causation yet
