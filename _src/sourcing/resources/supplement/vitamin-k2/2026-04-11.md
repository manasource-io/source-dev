## Claims Reviewed

Two Reddit posts (416 and ~400 upvotes) about vitamin K2 existed in the result pool but no resource was present. The posts consistently highlighted the Rotterdam Study, D3+K2 synergy, and MK-7 as the preferred daily form.

Validated three primary sources:
- PMID 15514282 (Rotterdam Study): strong observational evidence; 57% lower CV mortality, 41% lower CHD risk in highest K2 tertile.
- PMC8596038 (2021 narrative review): MGP mechanism well-described; acknowledges limited RCT data but mechanistically coherent.
- PMC9403798 (2022 meta-analysis of 16 RCTs): K2 improves lumbar BMD especially combined with D3/calcium; fracture evidence mixed.
