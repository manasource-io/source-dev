# Curcumin — Research Notes

- **Resource**: `resources/nutrition/supplements/curcumin.md`
- **Leads**: Web search on curcumin anti-inflammatory, bioavailability, joint pain, depression 2024–2025

## Claims Reviewed

Original stub was accurate but very thin. Existing examine.com reference is valid and strong.

Key findings confirmed and added:

- Bioavailability: standard curcumin is essentially inert without piperine or enhanced formulation (2,000% bioavailability increase with piperine)
- Joint pain: multiple meta-analyses confirm non-inferiority to NSAIDs for knee OA; fewer GI side effects
- Depression: CANMAT guidelines provisionally recommend 500–1,000 mg/day as adjunctive MDD treatment
- Anti-inflammatory: inhibits NF-κB, IL-6, TNF-α, COX-2 — overlapping NSAID mechanism
