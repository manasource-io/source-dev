## Notes

`benefitLevel: 8` is well-supported — magnesium is an essential nutrient with deficiency linked to multiple major chronic diseases. `credibility: strong` because the evidence base is extensive across prospective studies, meta-analyses, and mechanistic research. The biggest caveat is that supplementation benefits are most pronounced in people who are deficient; healthy people with adequate dietary intake see smaller gains.
