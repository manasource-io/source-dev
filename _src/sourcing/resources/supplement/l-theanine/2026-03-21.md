# L-Theanine — Research Notes

- **Resource**: `resources/nutrition/supplements/l-theanine.md`
- **Leads**: Web search on L-theanine + caffeine systematic reviews 2024–2025

## Claims Reviewed

Original stub was accurate. Examine.com reference is valid and well-sourced.

Key nuances added:

- Relaxation via alpha-wave increase (EEG-confirmed): reliable
- Caffeine synergy: most consistent benefit; 2:1 theanine:caffeine ratio well-studied
- Anxiety reduction: consistent for stressed populations; more modest in healthy non-stressed adults
- Sleep: 2025 systematic review confirms effect in stress-related sleep disruption but less clear in general population

Notable finding: 2025 _Nutrition Reviews_ meta-analysis (64 studies) found effects on cognition are "mostly driven by caffeine" — theanine's independent cognitive benefit is smaller than sometimes claimed.
