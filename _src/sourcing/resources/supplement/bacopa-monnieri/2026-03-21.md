# Bacopa Monnieri — Research Notes

- **Resource**: `resources/nutrition/supplements/bacopa-monnieri.md`
- **Leads**: Web search for recent clinical evidence

## Claims Reviewed

- Memory improvement: some evidence for free recall; inconsistent across other cognitive domains in systematic reviews
- Stress reduction: confirmed in 2025 RCT (stress reactivity, p=0.03) even where cognitive outcomes were null
- Cognitive speed/attention: meta-analysis supports modest improvement in attention speed
- Neuroprotection: mechanistically confirmed (antioxidant, anti-apoptotic, synaptic repair) in 2024 systematic review; clinical translation limited
- Alzheimer's disease: insufficient evidence — 2022 systematic review rated evidence quality as very low
