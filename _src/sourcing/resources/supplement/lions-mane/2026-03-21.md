## Claims Reviewed

- NGF/BDNF stimulation: well-established mechanistically; hericenones (fruiting body) and erinacines (mycelia) have distinct but complementary pathways
- Cognitive enhancement: rapidly improving human trial evidence — pilot RCT 2023 (Stroop task), 2025 RCT (fruiting body extract), 2025 systematic review (5 RCTs, MMSE +1.17)
- Mood/anxiety: supported by a 2010 human trial and multiple narrative reviews; plausible via hippocampal NGF pathway
- Alzheimer's potential: promising preclinical + a 2025 narrative review; not yet established in clinical trials
- Neuroprotection in Parkinson's, stroke: preclinical only
