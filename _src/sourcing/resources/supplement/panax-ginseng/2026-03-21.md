## Claims Reviewed

The existing stub was accurate but minimal (single sentence). The Examine.com reference is valid and comprehensive: 67 references, 63 trials, 3 meta-analyses, 9,886 participants, with B-level evidence for cognition and mood, and 18+ glucose trials.

Two additional peer-reviewed sources were added:

- PMC6874434: Drosophila lifespan extension via Korean red ginseng / dSir2 and insulin/IGF-1 signaling
- PMC11222675: 2024 mouse study on anti-aging gut microbiome effects of Panax ginseng extract

These establish mechanistic longevity evidence consistent with known anti-aging pathways (sirtuin activation, IGF-1 modulation) — important context for a longevity app.
