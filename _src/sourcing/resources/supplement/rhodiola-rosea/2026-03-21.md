## Claims Reviewed

- Adaptogenic stress reduction: well-supported by multiple trials and EMA endorsement (2011)
- Fatigue reduction: supported by SHR-5 extract RCTs and open-label chronic fatigue trial (200 mg BID × 8 weeks)
- Exercise performance: ~16 human investigations; modest ergogenic effect acute (~200 mg, 60 min pre-exercise)
- Cognitive support under stress: consistent across trials; less reliable at rest
- Longevity: plausible via Hsp70/JNK-1/DAF-16 stress-resistance pathway but no direct human longevity data
