## Claims Reviewed

Psyllium husk is one of the most clinically studied dietary fiber supplements. The evidence for LDL reduction via bile acid binding is strong and has FDA recognition. Blood sugar stabilization via viscous fiber gel formation is also well-supported across RCTs. The Mount Sinai reference covers these well.
