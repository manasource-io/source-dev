# Sauna Usage — Research Notes

- **Resource**: `resources/exercise/sauna.md`
- **Leads**: Web search for Laukkanen Finnish sauna cohort studies 2024

## Claims Reviewed

- Cardiovascular mortality: confirmed — 50% reduction at 4-7x/week vs 1x/week (KIHD cohort, JAMA Internal Medicine 2015)
- All-cause mortality: confirmed — 40% reduction at 4-7x/week (same cohort)
- Both sexes: confirmed — 2018 BMC Medicine study extended findings to women
- Inflammation: confirmed — 2023 cohort study shows additive protection (low CRP + frequent sauna)
- Blood pressure: confirmed — passive heat exposure induces cardiovascular adaptations similar to exercise; reduces arterial stiffness
- Stroke, dementia, Alzheimer's: confirmed from Finnish cohort data; not in current references but established in the literature
