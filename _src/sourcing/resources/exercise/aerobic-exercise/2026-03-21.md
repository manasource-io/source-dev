# Aerobic Exercise — Research Notes

- **Resource**: `resources/exercise/aerobic-exercise.md`
- **Leads**: Web search for cardiorespiratory fitness and all-cause mortality meta-analyses 2023-2024

## Claims Reviewed

- All-cause mortality reduction: confirmed — 53% lower mortality (high vs low CRF) across 20.9M observations, 199 cohort studies (PMC11103301)
- Per-1-MET increase: 11-17% mortality reduction — strong dose-response
- Cardiovascular health: confirmed across extensive meta-analytic literature
- Mental health: confirmed; 2006 reference valid but narrow (letter, mental health focus only)
- Metabolic: confirmed; CRF attenuates obesity-related mortality risk ("fit and fat" effect)
