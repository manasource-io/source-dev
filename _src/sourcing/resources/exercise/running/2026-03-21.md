# Running — Research Notes

- **Resource**: `resources/exercise/running.md`
- **Leads**: Web search for running mortality meta-analyses; existing PMID 28365296 confirmed valid

## Claims Reviewed

- All-cause mortality: confirmed — 25-40% reduced premature mortality (Lee 2017 review); Pedisic 2019 meta-analysis: 14 studies, 232K participants, confirmed all-cause reduction
- CVD mortality: confirmed — 45% lower in Aerobics Center Longitudinal Study; 50% lower in persistent runners
- Dose-response: confirmed — mortality benefits similar across quintiles of running time/distance/frequency; threshold very low (<51 min/week, <6 miles)
- Life expectancy: confirmed — ~3 years longer life for runners vs non-runners
- Mental health: confirmed — runner's high is endocannabinoid-mediated (not endorphin); antidepressant-comparable effects in RCTs
- Joint safety: confirmed — recreational runners have LOWER rates of knee osteoarthritis than non-runners (contrary to common belief)
- Cancer mortality: confirmed — Pedisic 2019 meta-analysis also found reduced cancer mortality in runners
