# High-Intensity Interval Training — Research Notes

- **Resource**: `resources/exercise/high-intensity-interval-training.md`
- **Leads**: Web search for HIIT, cardiovascular health, VO2max, longevity 2023-2024 systematic reviews

## Claims Reviewed

- VO2max improvement: confirmed — 4×4 HIIT shows 10% gain vs 3.1-3.3% for MICT in RCT; superior in controlled trials for older adults
- Cardiometabolic health: confirmed — 2023 meta-analysis of 97 RCTs, 3399 participants supports HIIT for clinical cardiometabolic management
- Time efficiency: confirmed — shorter sessions achieve comparable/superior VO2max gains
- Safety: confirmed — landmark Norwegian data shows low absolute risk even in cardiac populations
- Longevity/mortality link: indirect (via VO2max → mortality reduction); no direct RCT of HIIT vs mortality as endpoint
