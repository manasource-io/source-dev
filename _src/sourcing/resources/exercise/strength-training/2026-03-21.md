# Strength Training — Research Notes

- **Resource**: `resources/exercise/strength-training.md`
- **Leads**: Web search for resistance training and longevity/mortality meta-analyses

## Claims Reviewed

- All-cause mortality: 10–21% reduction confirmed across multiple meta-analyses; 40% reduction when combined with aerobic exercise
- Muscle mass / sarcopenia: well-established; progressive resistance training is the primary intervention
- Bone density: confirmed across meta-analyses; especially important for postmenopausal women
- Metabolic health: 17% diabetes risk reduction; consistent improvements in insulin sensitivity and blood lipids
- Dose-response: J/U-shaped curve — optimal ~30–60 min/week; diminishing returns above 150 min/week
