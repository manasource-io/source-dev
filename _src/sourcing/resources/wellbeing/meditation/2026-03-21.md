# Mindfulness Meditation — Research Notes

- **Resource**: `resources/wellbeing/meditation.md`
- **Leads**: Web search for mindfulness meditation meta-analyses 2023-2024

## Claims Reviewed

- Anxiety reduction: confirmed — Goyal et al. 2014 (47 RCTs, 3515 participants); ES 0.38 at 8 weeks; moderate evidence
- Depression reduction: confirmed — ES 0.30 at 8 weeks; maintained at 3-6 months (ES 0.23)
- Stress reduction: confirmed — reduced cortisol reactivity, amygdala activation, prefrontal changes after 8 weeks
- Pain management: confirmed — ES 0.33; works on affective rather than sensory component
- Digital apps effective: confirmed — 2024 meta-analysis of 43 RCTs; g=0.24 depression, g=0.28 anxiety (smaller but real)
- Equivalent to CBT for mild-moderate depression: supported
- Cognitive improvement (broader): low evidence per Goyal et al.; not included as a primary benefit
