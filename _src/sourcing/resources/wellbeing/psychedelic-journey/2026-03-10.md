## Claims Reviewed

The Reddit post linked to a 2025 Nature journal study (npj Aging) showing psilocybin extends cellular lifespan in human fibroblasts and improves survival in aged mice. This is the "first experimental evidence" of psilocybin's anti-aging effects, according to the authors. The existing resource only covered mental health benefits (depression, anxiety) without this longevity angle.

Key references from the Reddit post:

- **Nature npj Aging 2025**: https://www.nature.com/articles/s41514-025-00244-x — "Psilocybin treatment extends cellular lifespan and improves survival of aged mice." Used psilocin on IMR-90 human lung fibroblasts; delayed senescence and preserved telomere length. Monthly psilocybin in 19-month-old mice improved survival at 10 months follow-up. First study of its kind.
- **PubMed 31634774**: The "psilocybin-telomere hypothesis" — precursor theoretical paper
- **PubMed 40628762**: PubMed record of the above npj Aging study

The existing mental health references (Nature Medicine 2022, PubMed 30392758) were verified as valid and strong.
