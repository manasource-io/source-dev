# Social Connection — Research Notes

- **Resource**: `resources/wellbeing/social-connection.md`
- **Leads**: Web search for Holt-Lunstad meta-analyses, social isolation and mortality

## Claims Reviewed

- 50% increased survival odds with strong social ties: confirmed — Holt-Lunstad 2010, 148 prospective studies, OR=1.50 (95% CI 1.42-1.59)
- Effect size comparable to smoking: confirmed by the same meta-analysis; this comparison is methodologically valid
- Social isolation increases mortality: confirmed — 2025 meta-analysis: +35% all-cause mortality; loneliness alone +14%
- Mental health: well-established, mechanistically clear
- Immune function: confirmed — loneliness drives inflammation (elevated IL-6, TNF-α, CRP), impaired NK cell function
- Cognitive protection in aging: supported across observational data, centenarian studies
