# Contact with Nature — Research Notes

- **Resource**: `resources/wellbeing/contact-with-nature.md`
- **Leads**: Web search for nature exposure health outcomes 2023–2024; also verified existing references

## Claims Reviewed

- Stress reduction (cortisol, HRV): confirmed — Shinrin-Yoku systematic review (64 studies, 2017): 80% increase in parasympathetic HRV indicators; reduced cortisol, heart rate
- Mental health (depression/anxiety): confirmed — 2024 meta-analysis (59 studies): OR 0.91 for psychiatric disorders; 29% lower depression odds (garden access), 27% lower anxiety odds
- Cardiovascular (diastolic BP): confirmed — Twohig-Bennett & Jones 2019 (103 observational + 40 interventional): -1.97 mmHg diastolic BP, reduced CVD mortality
- NK cell / immune function: confirmed — phytoncide studies by Qing Li; 50% NK cell activity increase post-forest trip; persists 7 days; replicated in hotel room phytoncide inhalation
- Dose-response: some data — 30 min/week threshold; 7% fewer depression cases and 9% fewer hypertension cases at population level if threshold met
- Dementia, schizophrenia, ADHD from green space: emerging, included in meta-analysis but effect sizes smaller and evidence quality lower
