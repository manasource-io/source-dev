# Sleep Quality — Research Notes

- **Resource**: `resources/restoration/sleep-quality.md`
- **Leads**: Web search for sleep duration, quality, and mortality meta-analyses 2023-2025

## Claims Reviewed

- All-cause mortality: confirmed U-shaped relationship; 7hr optimal; imbalanced sleep raises mortality 14-34% (2025 meta-analysis)
- Cardiovascular: confirmed; poor sleep quality → +44% coronary heart disease risk; Mendelian randomization supports causal link to CAD
- Metabolic: confirmed; insulin resistance, obesity, T2D associations well-established; sleep now in cardiovascular health metrics
- Cognitive/glymphatic: confirmed; amyloid-beta clearance during sleep; memory consolidation during slow-wave and REM sleep
- Long sleep as risk: confirmed but partly a disease marker — doesn't mean more sleep causes harm above 9hr
